# Monetization

### How to price your game
- Find similar genre games to your own game. See their prices. Put down the range to have some sort of understanding of the current market.
- Ask feedback from Beta testers "How much are they willing to pay for such game?"
- You can make a quick vid of your game and make a survey for online users (or friends), but if you go for friend route - your statistics will be unpredictable and not reliable due to bias.
- If you are a fan to play games yourself - you can analyze yourself what kind of games were not worth their price.
- Ask yourself "For how much am I willing to buy such game?"
